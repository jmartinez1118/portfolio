ABOUT

Humberto Beccera is an editorial portrait and music photographer residing in Columbia, South Carolina. As a former musician, he played with bands such as I Nine, Bryson Jennings, and Hannah Miller. As a natural creative, his love for photography as a form of expression became his ultimate path and career in 2007. His experience as a musician and his unique art background made it obvious that he should pursue music and editorial shoots as his primary focus as a photographer.
